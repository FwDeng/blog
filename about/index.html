About Me

Short Bio

I am a lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Technology in Shandong Technology and Business University (SDTBU).

Before joining SDTBU, I worked as an algorithm research engineer at Alibaba Cloud Computing Co. Ltd. I got my PhD from Tongji University in 2021. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Tongji University majoring in Transportation Engineering in 2016. Furthurmore, I participated in a two-month short-term research program at Tongji-MIT City Science Lab in summer of 2019.

Research Interests

My research interests center around urban computing and intelligent transportation systems, with a focus on developing innovative methodologies and applications to address efficiency and sustainability challenges within transportation systems. Specifically, I am passionate about integrating cutting-edge technologies into urban perception, agent-based simulation, and traffic flow modeling in order to drive impactful solutions for todayâ€™s complex transportation challenges.
